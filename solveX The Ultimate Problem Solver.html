SolveX Documentation

1. SolveX NLP (Word Problems)

**How to use:** Enter your full word problem (e.g., "Calculate the molarity of a solution...") or conceptual question. The engine processes the natural language, identifies the physics/chemistry/math concepts, and returns a detailed, explanatory solution.
Powered by SolveX Natural Language Processing.

2. SolveX Compute (Algebra & Calculation)

**How to use:** Enter a focused mathematical expression or equation (e.g., "integrate x^2 dx from 0 to 1," or "solve 3x + 5 = 14"). The system executes the exact computation and returns the final answer or simplified expression.
Powered by a SolveX Computation Engine .

3. SolveX Graph (Visualization)

**How to use:** Enter an equation you wish to plot (e.g., "y = e^-x * cos(5x)" or "x^2 + y^2 = 25"). The system converts the equation into a plot, which is displayed directly in the output panel for visual analysis.
Powered by a Dynamic Graphing Tool like Desmos.
